Welcome to official NSU virutal campus simulator app. THis is a unity 3D engine based virtual campus app that works in all mobile platforms. We have used technologies like: Google Sketchup 3D max and maya motion track via kinect This technologies helped us to create exact replica of actual campus of North South University.
